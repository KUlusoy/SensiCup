About SensiCup

SensiCup is a smart cup that enables you to get instant feedback about the cleanliness of your water using sensor data and machine learning. Unlike existing solutions, SensiCup is portable, practical, and fast. Rather than spending thousands of dollars to send samples to a lab and waiting weeks for results, SensiCup provides real-time water quality analysis with no extra charge.

Our Story

Some of our teammates reported seeing suspicious green buildup in their school's water fountains. Many students from the same schools have fallen ill after consuming contaminated water. From this experience, we were inspired to develop a device that alerts users about the purity (or impurity) of their water.
